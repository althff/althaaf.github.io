Here's a time line of Ir. soekarno' life:

  • 1901 - Born in Surabaya, Indonesia
  • 1916 - Finishes university
  • 1920 - he was dating Siti Oetari and they finally got married in 1920
  • 1923 - he divorces Siti and marries Inggit. They lasted twenty years but never had children so Soekarno divorced them in 1943
  • 1943 - His third wife was a teenager named Fatmawati. They were blessed with five children: Megawati Sukarnoputri (Indonesia's fifth president), Guruh Sukarnoputra, Rachmawati Soekarnoputri and Sukmawati Sukarnoputri.
  • 1943 - Soekarno together with Bung Hatta declare independence from the Netherlands and other countries for Indonesia. and became Indonesia's first president
  • 1944 - Soekarno died at the age of 69 in Jakarta, Indonesia.
  • 1945 - Discovers a way to grown wheat twice each season, doubling wheat yields
  • 1970 - crosses a short, sturdy dwarf breed of wheat with a high-yeidling American breed, creating a strain that responds well to fertalizer. It goes on to provide 95% of Mexico's wheat.
